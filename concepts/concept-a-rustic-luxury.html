A Tree That Tells a Story

At the center of The Sycamore stands its namesake — a majestic sycamore tree, carefully preserved and integrated into the cabin's architecture. Rising from the main floor through a hand-carved live-edge table, it serves as both sculpture and conversation piece, connecting the warmth of the cabin to the wildness of the forest outside.
