The Centerpiece

A tree that became a room

At the heart of The Sycamore stands its namesake — a full sycamore tree, stripped of bark, treated by hand, and integrated into the cabin's architecture. It rises from the floor through a live-edge table, climbing toward the vaulted ceiling. It is sculpture. It is structure. It is the reason you came.

The living room anchors around a hand-laid river stone fireplace with a natural wood mantel. Dark leather, warm light, the crackle of fire against cold mountain air. This is the room where you stop checking your phone.
